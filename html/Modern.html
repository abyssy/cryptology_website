Modern Crypto

Modern cryptography is a remarkable discipline. It is a cornerstone of computer and communicationssecurity, with end products that are imminently practical. Yet its study touches on branches of mathematics that may have been considered esoteric, and it brings together fields like number theory, computational-complexity theory, and probabiltity theory.
